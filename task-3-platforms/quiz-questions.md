# Digital Literacy Quiz Questions

## Question 1: Multiple Choice  
What does the term 'phishing' refer to?  
A) A method to catch fish  
B) An attempt to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity  
C) A computer virus  
D) None of the above  

### Answer: B

---

## Question 2: Short Answer  
What is the purpose of a firewall in computer networks?

### Answer:
To establish a barrier between a trusted internal network and untrusted external networks.

---

## Question 3: Multiple Choice  
Which of the following is a secure password?
A) password123  
B) 123456  
C) G7v!nB3@eZx0  
D) qwerty  

### Answer: C

---

## Question 4: Short Answer  
Define what digital literacy means in your own words.

### Answer:
Digital literacy refers to the ability to effectively use digital technology, communication tools, and networks to access, manage, integrate, evaluate, and create information.

---

A) Uniform Resource Locator  
B) Universal Resource Link  
C) Uniform Resource Link  
D) None of the above
