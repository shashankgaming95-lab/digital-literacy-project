# Reflection on Digital Literacy Infographic Creation

## Tools Used
- **Canva**: I utilized Canva for its user-friendly interface and diverse templates. It allowed me to create a visually appealing infographic with ease.
- **Google Scholar**: To gather credible information and references for my infographic, I searched academic articles related to digital literacy.
- **Trello**: I used Trello for planning and organizing my ideas and content before starting the design process.

## Design Topics Covered
- **What is Digital Literacy?**: I defined digital literacy and its significance in today's digital world.
- **Key Skills**: The infographic highlighted key digital skills, such as information literacy, communication abilities, and technical proficiency.
- **Resources**: I included links to helpful resources and tools aimed at enhancing digital skills.
- **Best Practices**: Emphasized the importance of critical thinking when assessing online information.

## Learning Experience
Creating the digital literacy infographic was both enlightening and challenging. I learned how to effectively condense information into visual formats and the importance of clear messaging. The process improved my research skills, as I scoured various resources for accurate content. Additionally, working with design tools like Canva provided me with new skills in graphic design, which I found quite rewarding. Overall, this project deepened my understanding of digital literacy and its importance in the modern world.
