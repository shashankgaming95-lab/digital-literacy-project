# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Do's:
1. **Do maintain privacy settings:** Adjust your profiles to limit who can see your personal information.
2. **Do think before you post:** Reflect on how your content might be perceived and its potential impact.
3. **Do engage positively:** Contribute constructively to discussions and support peers.
4. **Do verify your sources:** Share information only from credible sources to avoid spreading misinformation.
5. **Do stay professional:** Use appropriate language and images, especially in academic or professional contexts.

## Don'ts:
1. **Don't overshare personal information:** Avoid sharing sensitive details that could compromise your safety.
2. **Don't engage in online arguments:** Steer clear of contentious discussions that can escalate.
3. **Don't post when emotional:** Wait until you're calm before sharing thoughts that might reflect poorly on you.
4. **Don't tag others without consent:** Ask permission before tagging peers in photos or posts.
5. **Don't forget digital footprints:** Remember that online actions can have lasting consequences, so act responsibly.
